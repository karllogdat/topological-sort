    How to Use CS Curriculum Navigator

    1. Browse Subjects

    Click on any of the 5 main subjects to see all related topics. These cover CS 2nd year and below curriculum.

    2. Select Topics

    Choose specific topics from the subject list to focus your learning.

    3. Search Functionality

    Use the search bar to quickly find specific topics across all subjects.

    4. Learning Path

    The system will help organize your learning sequence based on the selected topics.

    This tool is designed specifically for Computer Science students in their 2nd year and below.
